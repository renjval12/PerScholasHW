About Me

Hey, I'm Renee!

My full name is ReneeJoy Valentine, but you can call me Renee (they/them). Some basic facts about me are that I'm 27, my favorite color is blue, my favorite flower is a Sunflower, and I'm a Capricorn for anyone that's into that sort of thing.

I've always identified myself as more of a "left-brainer", than a right one. I paint, make music, shoot films, write screenplays, and I'm generally pretty creative-minded. So, although, I've always loved tech, for a long time, I didn't think of myself as someone who belonged in the world of tech. However, today I think tech is the perfect place for me to be as a creative. Technology, much like art, is at the forefront of creativity and innovation. Sure, the George Orwells and H.G. Wells of the world dreamt of hoverboards and self-driving cars, but software engineers, and engineers alike, made those prophecies a reality. I'm hoping through what I learn from Per Scholas, I'll eventually have a chance to make my own innovative and creative, dream come true.
